TOGAF Exam & Certification

You must pass both TOGAF 9 level 1 (Foundation) and level 2 (Certification) exams to become TOGAF 9 certified. An exam voucher will be provided to you with the course, and you may take the exam at your convenience from any of the nearest Open Group Pearson VUE test centers.

There are no prerequisites to take the TOGAF certification exam.

1. Attend one complete batch.
2. Complete one simulation test with a minimum score of 60%.
3. Complete 85% of the TOGAF course.
4. Complete one simulation test with a minimum score of 60%.

Your preparation requirements for passing the TOGAF Certification exam will depend on your current role and your understanding of IT enterprise architecture. However, structured training like this TOGAF course will arm you with the right information to be assured of positive results.

Your TOGAF Certification exam voucher fee is included in the price of this TOGAF® course. However, you should avail of the exam voucher within six months from the date of TOGAF® course purchase.
Once you complete your training course within 6 months from the program start date, you will become eligible to unlock and claim the Exam Voucher from your LMS portal. In- case, if you are not able to complete the program in 180 days i.e 6 months from the program start date, you can request an extension of 90 days, in which you can complete the training course and can then unlock and avail the Exam voucher from your LMS portal. If you fail to request an extension of 90 days after failing to complete the training course in 180 days. You will receive reminder emails to request an extension of 90 days and complete the course in that time to become eligible for an exam voucher and avail of it.
If you still do not request for extension and still haven't completed your training course at the end of 365 days from your program start date, Exam vouchers will be sent to you directly on your LMS portal with email communication. The exam voucher comes with a validity of 1 year, and you will have to schedule/complete your exam within 1 year.

Yes, the TOGAF course fee is inclusive of the examination fee.

Usually you will receive notification of your pass or fail status within a few minutes of completing your exam in the form of a printed score report. Key enhancements made in this version include updates to the Business Architecture and the Content Metamodel.
