Eligibility

The PRINCE2 Foundation training program is ideal for current or aspiring senior project managers. This certification is particularly applicable for project managers in India, Australia, Europe, and for professionals working on projects in those markets. The course is best suited for project managers, assistant project managers, team leads, project executives, software developers, and any professional aspiring to be a project manager.

Pre-requisites

There are no prerequisites to apply for the PRINCE2 Foundation exam. However, it is recommended that you have at least two years of experience in a project management environment before pursuing this certification.
