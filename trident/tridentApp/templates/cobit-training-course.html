COBIT 2019 Exam Certification

  • To pass the COBIT® 2019 Foundation exam, the candidate should have a thorough knowledge of all the COBIT principles, design factors, and governance components.
  • One must possess experience in providing IT service and should have donned various roles in Enterprise IT to be able to understand and relate to the concepts covered in the COBIT course. There is no other prerequisite for this course.
  • The online examination consists of 75 multiple-choice questions and requires a score of 65% or higher to pass and 120 minutes are allowed for completing the exam.
  • Once the online examination is completed, a score with “Pass” or “Fail” is generated.

No formal qualification is required to take this COBIT course or complete the exam, other than the experience and knowledge of IT service-providing firms or IT service-providing departments of any firm.

FAQs

The COBIT® 2019 Foundation exam comprises 50 multiple-choice questions that are to be completed in 40 minutes. A total of 50% (25 out of 50) is required to pass, and it is a closed book exam. Computer-based testing is the standard method for COBIT exams.

After passing the exam, the names of successful candidates will appear in the Successful Candidate Register (if candidates have opted to register). The certificate will be sent to candidates at their correspondence addresses.

The COBIT® 2019 qualifications are valid for an indefinite period of time and do not expire, so there is no need to renew the certificate once you pass the exam.
