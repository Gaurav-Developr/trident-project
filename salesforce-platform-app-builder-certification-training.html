Agile Scrum Master Exam & Certification

To become a Certified Salesforce App Builder, you must pass the Salesforce App Builder (DEV 401) Exam.

  • Step 1: Complete this training course.
  • Step 2: Visit certification.salesforce.com and register to take the DEV 401 exam.
  • Step 3: Pass the exams to become certified.

There are no prerequisites for this course or to take the Salesforce DEV 401 examination.

  • Complete 85% of the course.
  • Complete 1 Project and simulation test with a minimum score of 60%.

Yes, we provide 1 practice test as part of our course to help you prepare for the actual certification exam. You can try this free Salesforce Platform App Builder Practice Test to understand the type of tests that are part of the course curriculum.

Learners taking the Salesforce Platform App Builder Certification exam should have proficiency in the following topics, each topic is provided with its corresponding exam weight:
  • Salesforce fundamentals - 8%
  • Data modeling and management - 20%
  • Security - 10%
  • Business logic and process automation - 27%
  • Social - 3%
  • User interface - 14%
  • Reporting - 5%
  • Mobile - 5%
  • App development - 8%

The exam is online-based, with a total of 60 multiple-choice questions. The test lasts for about 90 minutes. All candidates must get a minimum passing score of 68%.
